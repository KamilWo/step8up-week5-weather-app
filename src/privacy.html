Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes the city names you search for, which we use to provide weather forecasts. We do not collect personally identifiable information like your name or email address unless you voluntarily provide it through our contact form.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to operate, maintain, and provide you with the features and functionality of the Service. Search history may be stored locally on your device to improve your experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party APIs (like OpenWeatherMap) to provide weather data. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your data when you use our Service. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
